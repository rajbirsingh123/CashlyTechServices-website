Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started with Cashly

What is Cashly and how does it help mortgage agents?

Cashly is an AI-powered mortgage automation platform designed to reduce manual work for agents. It automatically fills out forms, sends follow-ups, and creates lender-ready summaries in seconds—saving you hours each week.

Is there a mobile version of Cashly available?

No, but Cashly is fully accessible via any mobile browser for your convenience.

How does Cashly automate borrower follow-ups?

Cashly triggers personalized email and SMS messages once borrower documents are received. This eliminates the need for manual reminders and improves conversion rates significantly.

Is borrower data stored securely on Cashly?

Absolutely. Cashly uses bank-grade encryption and complies with Canadian privacy laws to keep borrower data safe and confidential.

What kind of performance insights does Cashly provide?

Cashly offers real-time dashboards showing follow-up success rates, document submission timelines, deal approval metrics, and agent productivity insights.

How do I sign up for a Cashly account?

Visit our Contact section or click the “Get Started” button on the homepage to sign up. A Cashly onboarding specialist will help set up your account within minutes.
