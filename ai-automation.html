FAQ

Common questions before starting an AI workflow or automation project.

Most teams need clarity on where automation fits, what should stay human, and how to start without creating extra operational risk.

Do we need a full AI strategy before we start?

No. A repetitive workflow, handoff bottleneck, routing problem, or reporting burden is enough to identify a useful automation starting point.

Can you blend automation with existing systems?

Yes. We design around your CRM, forms, inboxes, APIs, internal tools, and approval flows so automation fits the stack you already use.

Do you only build chatbots?

No. We focus more broadly on operational automation, structured AI-assisted workflows, smart routing, and business logic that actually removes friction.

How do you keep AI features practical and explainable?

We keep decisions grounded in defined workflow rules, visible logic, and controlled inputs so teams can trust the output instead of guessing what happened.
