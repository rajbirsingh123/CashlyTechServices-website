CRM Strategy

How we approach platform design.

  • Map the pipeline, servicing, and reporting layers before writing product logic.
  • Design role-based views for managers, sales reps, support teams, and operations stakeholders.
  • Connect the CRM to email, telephony, documents, forms, and external data where needed.
  • Build governance into reminders, SLAs, approvals, status rules, and follow-up routines.
